The Maipuean Language Family

  1. Northern:
    1. Central Maipuran:
          Enawené-Nawé, Mehináku, Parecis, Waurá, Yawalapití
    2. Eastern Maipuran: Palikúr
    3. Mawayana: Mawayana  
    4. Northern Maipuran:
      1. Caribbean: Arawak, Garifuna, Wayuu
      2. Inland:
            Achagua, Baniwa, Cabiyari, Carútana, Curripaco, Guarequena,
            Mandanhuaca, Ipeka-Tapuia, Piapoco, Resígaro, Tariano, Yucuna
      3. Wapishana: Atorada, Mapidian, Wapishana
    5. Southern Maipuran:
      1. Bolivia-Parana:
            Baure, Ignaciano, Kinikinau, Paunaka, Terêna, Trinitario
      2. Pre-Andine:
            Asháninka, Ashéninka Perené, Ashéninka Pichis, Ashéninka Pajonal,   
            Ashéninka South Ucayali, Ashéninka Ucayali-Yurúa, Caquinte,
            Machiguenga, Nanti, Nomatsiguenga
      3. Purus:
            Apurinã, Iñapari, Machinere, Mashco Piro, Yine
    6. Western Maipuran: Chamicuro, Yanesha'
  2. Unclassified: Xiriâna
