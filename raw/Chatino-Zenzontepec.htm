Other comments: Zenzontepec Chatino has a vigesimal system similar to that of Tataltepec Chatino. The words from 16 to 19 are formed by adding the numbers 1 through 4 to a new base 15. There are Spanish loanword for hundred and thousand. Notes for phonological transcriptions between traditional symbols and IPAː a̜ = IPA [ã], u̜ = IPA [ũ], ʼ = IPA [ʔ], č = IPA [tʃ], š = IPA [ʃ].
