Other comments: Seme has a vigesimal system. Jula numbers are used for 30 and up.  Many Siamou do not know how to count in their language. Other important numbers

areː 160 'k͡pɛ̃́lk͡pɛn1nĩ12' ( 80 x 2 ), 400 'k͡pɛ̃́lkwɛ̃l3' ( 80 x 4 ) or 'hlɛ̃ mɔ byéẽ' and 800 'hlɛ̃3níĩ23'.
