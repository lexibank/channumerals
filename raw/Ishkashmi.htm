Other comments: Ishkashmi borrowed Tajiki numerals after ten now. There is a note in Pakhalina (1959:50) that the original Ishkashimi system starting from ten referred to hands: ‘ten’ - də dost (lit.: two hands), ‘eleven’; də dost ət uk (lit.: two hands and one)
