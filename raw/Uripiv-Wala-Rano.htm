Other comments: The above data was taken from Atchin dialect. Atchin has a decimal counting system. Numeral etes 'one ' is only used to represent the first single digit numeral when counting without a noun. For two decimal digits, and when used as a noun modifier, sa is used to represent 'one'.

roman is a linking word between the numeral fro tens and the numeral for unites.
