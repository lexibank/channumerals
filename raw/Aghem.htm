Other comments: The modern Aghem numeral system is decimal. There are option for the number 20 wu̞ːwu̝ etymologically means 'human body'), this means that Aghem might have a traditional numeral system based on twenty before. There is an option for 100 fə́fu̝ː used to count of money. Other higher numbers areː fə́kʷə́n for 1000.000 and bàŋ for 1000.000.000. Note that there are six tones in Aghemː High tone v́, Mid tone V̄, Low tone V̀, Falling tone V̂, Rising tone V̌ and High mid v.

Other comments: Aghem has a decimal system. The Aghem number 'twenty' is irregular and etymologically means 'body of a person' = 10 fingers and 10 toes. The remaining decades are forms by ŋ́-ʔɣɨ́m + the numbers 3 through 9.
