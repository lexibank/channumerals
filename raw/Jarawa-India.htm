Language name and locationː Jarawa, India [Refer to Ethnologue]

语言名称和分布地区: 贾拉瓦语, 印度安达曼群岛

 

1. wəyə

2. nayə

3. kaŋ itəjile

.   manyː mala-(wə)

 

Linguist providing data and dateː Prof. Juliette Blevins, Director, Endangered Language Initiative Linguistics Program, CUNY Graduate Center, 365 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10016-4309, USA, January 27, 2012.

提供资料的语言学家: Prof. Juliette Blevins, 2012 年 1 月 27 日.

 

Other comments: Jarawa has a simple 'one', 'two', 'three', 'many' system. The word for many is 'mala-(wə)'.
