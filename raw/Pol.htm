Other comments: Pol has a decimal system. Pomo is spoken in Sangha Region, north of Ouesso, on borders with Cameroon and Central African Republic. Not in Central African Republic. Alternate names: Pol, Pori, Pul, Congo Pol. Pomo has a decimal system. Please note that the ''tç'' in number 6 is a voiceless palatal plosive.
