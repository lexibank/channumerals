Other comments: Kwa or Baa has a vigesimal system with special structures for 6, 7 and 9. Note that on the above table where there are question marks my consultants have come with different suggestions, i.e. on the numbers 200-1000. I hope to clarify this and will get back to you then. As mentioned earlier most speakers already switched to use Hausa numbers rather than Baa.
