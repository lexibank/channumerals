Other comments: Upper Tanana is an endangered language. 11 tsʼehłaɡn deetaan uk’et tsʼehłaɡn means ''ten on top of it, one''). For Upper Tanana (Northway Dialect) use the followingː

   tsʼehłeɡn in place of Tetlin's tsʼehłaɡn, laakay in place of Tetlin's laakey, otherwise the two dialects are the same.

Note that the Phonetic transcriptions between IPA and phonetic Americanist ː

   1. ł = IPA [ɬ], 2. tsʼ =IPA [tsʼ], voiceless ejective affricate.
