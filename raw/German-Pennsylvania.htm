Other comments: Pennsylvania Dutch or Pennsylvania German is spoken by about 148,00 speakers scattered in Florida; Indiana; Iowa; Kansas; Missouri; Ohio; Oklahoma; Pennsylvania; Virginia; West Virginia; Wisconsin; new communities in other states in USA and 15,000 speakers  in Kitchener-Waterloo area, Ontario, Canada.
