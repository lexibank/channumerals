Other comments: In Faroese, the numbers 1-3 are inflected in four cases (nom., acc., gen., and dat.), three genders (masc., fem., and neut.) and two numbers (singular and plural). There are two sets of complex numbers 21 to 29, first set is more popular;

there are also two sets of decades, the first set is of Danish origin.
