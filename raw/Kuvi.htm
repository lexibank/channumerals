Other comments: Kuvi has a vegisimal system. Kuvi only remains two traditional Dravidian numerals 1 to 2. Numerals from three to twenty are expressed by using the Oriya numerals.  The Kuvi language people group are living in the Koraput and Malakangiri district of Odisha in India.

Other comments: Kuvi only remains two traditional numerals 1 to 2. Numerals from three to ten are expressed by using the Oriya numerals with the classifier -goʈʈa, numerals from eleven to twenty are expressed by using the Oriya numerals with or without the classifier -goʈʈa.  Twenty is salga, but they borrow Oriya word koːɖe to construct compound numerals from 21 to 29.
