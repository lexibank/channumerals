Other comments: Koorete has a decimal system, the word thousands 'kúmà' and 'ʃíʔɛ̀' are both loan words, the first from Guji-Oromo, the second from Amharic. the 'tsʼ' sound as in number 'hundred' is an dento-alveolar affricated ejective.
