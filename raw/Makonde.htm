Other comments: Makonde has a quinary, decimal numeral system. Nowadays, the numerals from 6 onwards employs Swahili numerals. Note that accent mark indicates high tone and low tone unmarked and also that /nn/ is represents a long nasal (the result of reduction and desyllabification of the prefix mu- before a consonant-initial stem).
