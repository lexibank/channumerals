Other comments: Vɔmnəm [Vɔ̀mnə̄m] of Tuli is spoken by the Vɔ̀mní (= Koma Vomni), Northwestern part of the Alantika Mountains, Adamawa State, Nigeria

Vɔmnəm has a vigesimal system. The unit 20 is zuu  "head".

Vɔmnəm is a noun class language. Numbers from 1-4 may agree with the nouns they qualify.
