Other comments: Kabba has a decimal system with special form for numbers 8 and 9. Counting from below, to say ‘100’ they say ‘ten tens’/ ‘dodoku’. But they find this laborious for counting above 100 and so they switch to ‘kor kara’ to continue from there (e.g., 101 is ‘kor kara gire kara’). For 1,000 one can say ‘thousand’ / ‘doro’ by itself or be precise by saying the full ‘one thousand’ / ‘doro kara’.
