Other comments: The data is from the Wosera-Kamu dialect of Ambulas language, the

traditional counting system of this dialect is based on 'five', 2000 is expressed by ''vidik vidik tɑmbɑ vidik vidik tɑmbɑ tɑmbɑ''. However old data collected from Maprik dialect on the book by the late Glen A. Lean showed that the phrase ''maan vetik taaba vetik '', ''hands two and feet two'' was used to expressed 20.
