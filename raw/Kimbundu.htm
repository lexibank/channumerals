Other comments:  

  (1)  For the number "20" , with a translation in French (it is must easier for me!),

         word for word:
         mákwínyí á yàdì
         pl.10 + pronoun + two /dizaines + celles de + deux/ "les dizaines de deux"

         deux dizaines = 20

  (2)  For the numbers 30 to 90 in kimbundu, and in other languages also, the

        string /-i+a (pronoun)/ merged together, and a final number also:

        mákwínyí á tanù &#8594; mákwínyátánù (50).
