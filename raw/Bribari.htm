Other comments: Bribri has a decimal system. There are several possible words for 1, 2, 3 and 4, depending on the class of the noun to which it refers. The above list is used for human class, the generic class is the same with exceptions for three words 1, 2 and 3 marked with an asterisks. The numbers 'hundred' and 'thousand' are Spanish loanwords. The old form 'dabam döka̠ dabom éyök 'was not used now.
