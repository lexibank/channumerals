Other comments: There are three sets of cardinal numbers from one to ten in Kanakanabu. The first set is used in serial counting, the second for counting things and the third for counting human beings. Note that the traditional spellings: c = IPA [t͡s],

c = IPA [ɕ], ʉ = IPA [ɨ] .
