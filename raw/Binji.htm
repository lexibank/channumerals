Other comments: ciBinji [cìbìnjì cyà ngúsù) is spoken in Lukibu sector, Dimbelenge Territory, Kasaï Central province, Democratic Republic of Congo. ciBinji has a numeral system very similar to that of Luba-Kasai language.

Note that the traditional symbols: [c] is = IPA symbol [tʃ], and [j] = IPA symbol [dʒ]
