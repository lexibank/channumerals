Other comments: CiTonga or KiTonga has a traditional quinary system. Please note that

CiTong is spoken at Nkhata Bay District, Malawi and according to Professor Lee Bickmore it is a Zone N 15. language and this language is different from Tonga [toi] of Zimbabwe and Zambia and Tonga [toh].
