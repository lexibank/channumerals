Other comments: Kurtoep has an indigenous vigesimal system. However, from 20 beyond, speakers may use the Kurtoep numerals or the Dzhongkha numerals, but the informant said that only uneducated Kurtoep speakers use traditional numerals beyond twenty. The formations for traditional Kurtoep tens are similar to those of Tshangla (Motuo-Menba). The forms cíkɟɑ̀, ɲìɟɑ̀, and ɲìtóŋ are borrowings from Dzongkha. ɲìktóŋ is a Kurtöp innovation, based on analogy with the Dzongkha forms for ‘100’, ‘200’ and ‘1,000’
