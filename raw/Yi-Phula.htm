Other comments: Phula has a decimal system. Phula is a language in the Southeastern Ngwi group. Note that the forms of compound numerals 13 to 19, 22 to 29, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 200 and 2000 need to be checked with new data. Please note that there are two different forms for the number ''one''.
