Other comments: The Eipomek has a base–25 counting system (yupa = bundle), counting along arms and head (e.g. famu = thumb; barye = right side; tek = forearm bone; fin = inside of elbow; taku = shoulder; o = ear; selek = left side). Some words have no other meaning but are used only for counting, such as kok lom, which is the spot just behind the earlobe.
