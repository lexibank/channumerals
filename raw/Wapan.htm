Other comments: Wapan Jukun has a quinary, vigesimal numeral system.The word for twenty 'ádì zun' is an abbreviation of 'ádì pà zun', which means 'body person one'. They are also Hausa loanwords 'dèrí' and 'dubu' for 100 and 1000.
