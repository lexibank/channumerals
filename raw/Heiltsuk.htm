Other comments: Heiltsuk numeral system is decimal, note the /gi/ in 21 thru 29 is a proclitic consisting of a root {g-} “and further” and the least semantically marked one of a set of suffixes referring to the 3rd person. Please note that the phonetic symbols “m,” “n” and “l” with a dot underneath in numerals 1, 2, 11, 12, 20 to 29, 70, 80, 90 and 2000 indicates a vocalic pronunciation and a comma above the phonetic symbols indicates glottalization and an acute signal indicates high tone.
