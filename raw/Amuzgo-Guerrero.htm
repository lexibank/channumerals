Other comments: Guerrero Amuzgo has a vigesimal numeral system with special structure for 16 to 19. Hundreds and thousand are loanwords from Spanish. Note that the following differences between practical orthography and IPA transcriptionsː

1. c & qu = k, 2. The small raised n [vⁿ] indicates nasalization of the preceding vowel.

3. dy = dʲ, 4. ty = tʲ,   5. ñ = ɲ, 6. ch = tʃ, 7. y = j, 8. ' = ʔ.

The tones are expressed as the followsː

1.  ˧ = high tone, 2. ˦ = mid tone, 3. ˨ = low tone, 4. ˥˧= high-low glide.

5. ˩˧=low-mid glide
