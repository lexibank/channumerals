Other comments: Turkish numeral system is rather "regular" from 11 to 19: 10+1, 10-2, etc. (on bir, on iki etc. up to on dokuz for 19), departs from the "2 to 5" series from 20 to 50 with yirmi, otuz, kɪrk, elli, and returns to the 7, 8, 9 series for 70, 80, 90(altmɪş, yetmiş, seksen, doksan), and adds the units 1 to 9 to the decade numbers (yirmi bir, otuz bir, etc.).
