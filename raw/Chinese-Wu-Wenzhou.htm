Other comments: In Wenzhou Wu, the numerals 'one' and 'two' have two forms respectively, they are used in different positions.  Also, the form of numbers 'two' and 'five' are same, but the tone values are different. Wenzhou has a very complicated sound system and is unintelligible with Shanghainese.
