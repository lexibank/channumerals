Other comments: Bukusu adopted a numeral system mixing with traditional numbers with Swahili / Arabic borrowings. The traditional numbers retained areː ndala, tʃiβili ,

tʃitaru, tʃiraano, munaane and lííkʰumi. There are two sets of numerals after ten up to 90, but the borrowed numbers (first column) are more prevalent.
