Other comments: Balanta-Ganja has a decimal system with special substructure for 7 to 9. The numbers for 100 and 1000 might be loanwords from neighboring languages. Prefix agrees with class of noun counted. 'aa'' and 'ii' are lengthened and tight vowels.
