Other comments: Malo or Tamambo has a decimal system. Some of the numerals are similar to that of Polynesian languages, but the numerals from 11 to 19, 21 to 29 are

not in use, known only to 2 or 3 old people, English numbers used instead.

There are alternative numerals from 11 to 19 as below; this system originating from east Malo dialect, and known and used by same Tamambo speakers.

11. domana atea,  12. domana arua, 13. domana atolu, 14. domana aβati,

15. domana alima, 16. domana aiono, 17. domana abitu, 18. domana aβʷalu,

19. domana asuβʷa.
