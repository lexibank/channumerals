Other comments: The Yuchi numerical system is base on ten. The first column was provided by the late Dr. James Crawford (correspondence dated July 21, 1981) and the second from a manuscript English-Yuchi lexicon compiled by Dr. William L. Ballard. Yuchi 9 seems to be made up of parts of 1 and 3. In Ballard's source, Ballard says 100 'ʔyuštʼæ̜tʼe means 'rod one' and 1000 ʔyuštʼæ̜ʔætʼe means 'road big one'.
