Other comments: The traditional Uri system is a digit-tally one, according to Smith, and the first five numerals given above. There is a basic numeral set ( 1, 2, 3) or, possibly, (1,2) depending on where the numeral 3 can be regarded as a compound which contains the numerals 2. The numeral 4 and 5 have the constructions respectively'  2+ 2 ' and ' 2+ 2 + 1'. Smith gives two further sets of data collected from Uri informants which therefore probably unreliable. The system shown is most unusual and is worthy of further investigation. No details about it, however, were elicited from the informant. Numerals with an asterisk are taken from Smith (1984).
