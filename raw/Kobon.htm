Other comments: Kobon has a body-part tally systems with a 23-cycle. There are alternatives up to 4 (shown in italic type face). In the numerals beyond 23 there is an alternative of (shown with bold type face) using ñɨnjuöl (hands pulled out) for 20, and adding on the body parts tally numerals to produce 21-39, then ñɨnjuöl (hands pulled out) mɨhöp (two), i.e. 20 x 2 = 40, and adding on the body parts tally numerals to produce 41-59, etc, then ñɨnjuöl (hands pulled out) mɨhau nɨgaŋ (three), i.e. 20 x 3 = 60, and adding on the body parts tally numerals to produce 61-79, etc.
