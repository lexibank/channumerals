Other comments: Hamar has a vigesimal system, 20 éedi kála kaisá means 'one complete man' and 21 mean 'one complete man and one mouth', 40 is expressed by 'two complete men'. Number '1000' is an Amharic loanword. Note that Hamar is a pitch-accented language.
