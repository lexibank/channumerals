Other comments: The Suabo or Inanwatan count on hands and feet. Counting starts on the left little finger. The numerals from 1 to 4 reveal a binary system ( 3 = 2 + 1; 4= 2+ 2). The numerals 5 (one hand), 10 (both hands), and 20 (one body) are body-part based and combine with the numerals for 1-4 and with each other to form additive numeral phrases. Gesture tend to accompany the use of the numerals. The system is rapidly being replaced by Malay numerals.
