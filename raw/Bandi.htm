Other comments: Bandi has a decimal system with special construction for numbers

6 to 9. The number 9 might be etymologically formed by ' ten minus one '.

The numbers ' hundred' and ' thousand ' are borrowed from English with phonetic

modifications.

Noteː

   2) ítà(ŋ), hítà(ŋ), tààwú(ŋ), tààvú(ŋ) and pû(ŋ), púù(ŋ) are dialectal variants
