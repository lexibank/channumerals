Other comments: Pana is also called "Red Miao", is spoken by 1,000 people at villages of Shangpai, Zhongpai and Xiapai in Chengpu County and Suining County in Hunan Province, Southwest China. This language has been mentioned in the Ethnologue. After ten, Pana language often use numerals from local Chinese variety.
