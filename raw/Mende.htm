Other comments: Mende has a vigesimal system with special construction for numbers 6 to 9. The number 9 might be etymologically formed by 'ten minus one'. There are

two forms for number 'one', 'itáá' occurs in enumeration only. The numbers hundred and thousand are loanwords from English with phonological adaptations. Tone markings:  á = High tone and Low tone is unmarked.
