Other comments: Chumcho has a vigesimal system. There are alternative terms for 30 and 50ː 30ː sɯmtɕu "30 (3×10)" is a loan from Tibetan or nɪsau sa (20+10); ɖai "2,5" (in ɖai nɪsa "50") and hɐzar "1000" are loans from Indo-Aryan.
