Other comments: Aleut has a decimal system with hatxiχ ''ten'' and ''sisaχ'' hundred. In the above data was based on Unangan dialect in Russia. In Attuan and in the oldest Aktan and Eastern Aleut sources there are traces of a vigesimal system. The number ''five'' ca-/ cha- is derived from ''hand''.
