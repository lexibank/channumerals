Other comments: Generally, Oirat, Khalkh Mongolian and Buryat’s numeral system are

similar to each other.  However some numerals of Oirat have different pronunciation for phonetics. For example;

Oirat: dɔlaː/n   Khalkha and Buryat: dɔlɔː/n (seven)

Oirat: dʒir/n  Khalkha and Buryatː dʒara /n (sixty)

Oirat: miŋga/n  Khalkhaː mⁱaŋga /n (thousand)

Oirat numerals are more similar to Kalmyk.
