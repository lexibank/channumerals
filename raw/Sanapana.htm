Other comments: Sanapaná has developed counting system up to 2000. The numerical system above is, for the most part, a recent development. They normally use numbers 1-3 in their language and then Spanish numbers after that; twenty means 'two and one circle (0)', ʌkyʌkyema = 'circle'.

Their traditional system of counting is as follows:
