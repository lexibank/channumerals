Other comments: Phuie has a vigesimal numeral system. The are two options for 9, 19 and 29, the word 'tʰõ̀' means 'is lacking', so 29 can be expressed as 'mááɡí nì fí dùdúmí tʰõ̀' = ( 20 plus 10, one is lacking ). Phuien has high tone ( marked with an acute accent, á), low tone ( marked with grave accent, à), automatic downstep, also called downdrift (unmarked) and non-automatic downstep (marked with a superscripted exclamation point  ).
