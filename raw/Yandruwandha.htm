Language name and location: Yandruwandha, Australia [Refer to Ethnologue]

语言名称和分布地区: 扬德鲁万塔语, 澳大利亚西部

 

1. kurnu /kuɳu /

2. parrkulu /parkulu/

3. parrkukurnu /parkukuɳu / 

4. parrkulu-parrkulu 

5. mara /maɹa/

10. mara parrkulu

     ngalyi / ŋaʎi/ ' a few', malkirri /malkiri/ 'many'.

 

Linguist providing data and dateː Mr. Gavan Breen, Institute for Aboriginal Development Incorporated (IAD), Alice Springs, NT, Australia. November 2, 2012.

提供资料的语言学家: Mr. Gavan Breen, 2012 年 11 月 2 日.

 

Other comments: Traditionally Yandruwandha didn't use words for very higher numbers, so there are only two traditional numbers 1 and 2, and 5 mara which must be from the word 'hand'.
