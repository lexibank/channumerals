Other comments: Central Ifugao or Amganad Ifugao has a decimal system. They use

Spanish numbers after twenty often and have traditional tens for 40, 50, 60, 70, 80 and 90. Please not that word for 50 "na'boŋle" comes from the word for 50 bundles of rice. 25 bundles are duway dalan and 50 bundles are nabongle (root bongle).
