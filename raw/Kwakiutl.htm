Other comments: Kwakiutl or Kwakwala has a decimal system, but the structures for the compound numerals 11 to 19, 21 to 29, 31 to 39, 91 to 99 are particular. 31 is mamusɡ̌əmɡ̌ustola sə nʼəm, 45 is sayakʼəsmɡusto sə səkʼa and 99 is lalakʼəndala sə nˀanˀəma, 100. Please note that the following conversions between Americanist phonetic notation and IPAː 1. ɡ̌ =IPA [ɡʲ]?, 2. ƛ=IPA [tɬ], 3. ƛʼ=IPA [tɬʼ], 4.y=IPA[j].
