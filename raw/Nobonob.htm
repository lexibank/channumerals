提供资料的语言学家: Mr. & Mr. Ulys & Verna Aeschliman , 2011 年 6 月 2 日.

 

Other comments: The Nobonob has a system similar to that of Wagi language. The concept behind the numbers 6-10 is ''one hand and one, one hand and two'' etc. We are told that traditionally they used hands and feet in the same way to count from 11-20, but we’ve never heard any one use it naturally. Currently, for anything above 10, and often from 6-10 also, the Melanesian Pidgin as influenced by English counting system is used..
