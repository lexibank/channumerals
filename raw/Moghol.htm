Other comments: Owing to the strong influence of Persian and Arabic, the Mogholi compound numerals, decades, hundreds and thousands are very different from other Mongolian languages.  In the 1970s, when the German scholar Michael Weiers did fieldwork on the language, only a small number of people, mostly over the age of 40, knew the language, and in general, their knowledge of the language was passive. In all likelihood the Moghol language has fallen into desuetude.

The å used in Mogholi is pronounced as IPA [ɔ].
