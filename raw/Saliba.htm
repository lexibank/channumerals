Other comments: Saliba has a quinary counting system similar to that of Buhutu.

Now the preference is to use English numbers. The simple numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 10 and 20 are most likely to be used in Saliba. 100 and 1000 are loanwords, and are used

like countable nouns, e.g, yama labui 'two fish', yama tausani labui 'a tousand fish'.
