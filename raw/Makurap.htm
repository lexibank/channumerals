Other comments: Makuráp can count up to 20 with hand and foot.

Language name and locationː Makuráp, Brazil [Refer to Ethnologue]

语言名称和分布地区: 马库拉普语, 巴西

 

1. erekup

2. erea

3. erea kãjom

4. erea-erea ( 2 + 2 )

5. ki mot wut ( litː 'all our hand' )
6. ki mot ʃapẽrẽen papik ( litː 'our hand's other side caught' )
7. ki mot ʃapẽrẽen akuaj-et wareta-t ( litː 'our hand other side near by' )
8. ki mot piapuːret papik ( litː 'our hand's middle caught' )
9. ki mot piapuːret waretaːt akauj-et  ( litː 'other one near middle of our hand' )
10. ki mot wut wut  (litː 'our hand all all ' )
11. ki mit eri papik  (litː 'our foot -from -caught' )
15. ki mit ʃapẽrẽen ʃajwiin papik  (litː 'our foot -other side-small one-caught' )
20. ki mit ʃapẽrẽen wut  (litː 'all the other side of our foot' )

 

Linguist providing data and dateː Prof. Wolf Dietrich, University of Muenster, Germany, November 11, 2008.

提供资料的语言学家: Prof. Wolf Dietrich, 2008 年 11 月 11 日.

 

Other comments: Makuráp can count up to 20 with hand and foot.
