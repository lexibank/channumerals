Other comments: Mundani has a decimal systems. The 'ye-', 'yea-' and 'be' are noun class prefixes that may vary according to the noun class affiliation of object (s) being counted. Numeral rootsː -mɔʔ, -be, -tat, etc are not used in isolation.
