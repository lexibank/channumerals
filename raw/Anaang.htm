Other comments: Anaang has a numeral system similar to that of Efik. The alternative form for ten ùsʉ́k-kèed means ''ten less one''. There is a word for 400 ńdʉ̀, 500 is ńdʉ̀

m̀mè édíp ìtiên or ikiê ìtiên, and there is a loanword from English àtɔ́sìn for 'thousand'.
