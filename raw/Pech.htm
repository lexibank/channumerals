Other comments: Pech has a vigesimal system. Higher decades cardinals are formed through a multiplicative process employing the morpheme -pé- 'times' (which may be related to the adjective particle péː 'much, many'); e.g. kʷàra-pé-kàh  '10 x 4'='40'; kʷàra-pé-ùkkwa '10 x 10' = '100', etc. Pech is a tonal and please note that the v̀ indicates low tone and v́ high tone.
