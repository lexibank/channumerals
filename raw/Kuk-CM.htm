Other comments: Kuk [kʊ̀] has a decimal system similar to that of Kung and other related West Ring languages. This language is spoken at Naikom and Wum villages in North-West Province, Menchum Division, Fungom Subdivision, NWR Cameroun.

Note that there are two options for 200, fʊ̀lə́mbò is used for counting 200 francs.
