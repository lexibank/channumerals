Other comments: Befang has an unusual decimal system with 7 and 9 distort the counting base. So, is the language base 4 or 6 or is it still considered as base 10 ?

This language is the sister of Obang. The Befang and the Obang both originated from Widikum. But there are light changes in relation to the vowels and consonants in both languages.
