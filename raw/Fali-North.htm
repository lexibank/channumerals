Other comments: North Fali has  has decimal system with special construction for

numbers 8 and 9. The number 8 is a reduplication of náːn 'four'; and ŋɡʌskum is

etymologically derived from ŋɡʌs kàm(kàn) k͡pòlò 'rest hand one', which means

' its rests on of (two) hands'.  They might borrowed Fulani numbers for 'hundred' and

'thousand' now. The data need to check with the updated one.
