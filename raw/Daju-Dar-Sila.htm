Other comments: Dar Sila Daju has a decimal system similar to that of Dar Dadju. They might have borrowed Arabic numerals from 'hundred' and 'thousand'. Note that tones are used as follows  if you see high tone and rest are not marked means that all of them are high. So as a low  tone or neutral.
