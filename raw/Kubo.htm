Language name and location: Kubo, Papua New Guinea [Refer to Ethnologue]

提供资料的语言学家: Mr. Tom Covington, 2011 年 3 月 16 日.

 

Other comments: Kubo has a body-tally system count from left hand to right hand and up to 27. Now, most use this system for 1-5, but switch to English above that. Also, if counting thing, (1) and (2) are 'tanɔ' and beyãũ.
