Other comments: Alutiiq has an old vigesimal system but the new system is decimal. In old system, 11 was expresses by ''qula allrilumek atrarngaluni '' or ''qula allrilumek ciplluku'', 12 was expressed by ''qula malʔugnek atrarngaluni '' or ''qula malʔugnek ciplluku'', etc. The data was based on Koniag Alutiiq dialect of Kodiak Island.
