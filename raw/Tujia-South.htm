Other comments: Due to the strong influence from the Chinese, the Southern Tujia only has few traditional numbers, except for the loanwords from Chinese, the numerals 7, 8 and 9 are very particular and interesting phenomenon: they according to the meaning of local Chinese dialect and use own lexemes to describe these three Chinese Characters to express numbers 7, 8, 9:  for 7, they use Chinese Character七 which means '小钩 small hook'; for 8 use Chinese Character 八 which means '眼眉毛 eyebrows';  and for 9 use Chinese Character 九 which means '大钩 big hook'. Without the interpretation of local native speakers, ones cannot easily understand the original meanings.
