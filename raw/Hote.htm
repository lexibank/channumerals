Other comments: Hote has a quinary-vigesimal system. The word for five is derived from 'half hand' but the origin for ten 'laumiŋ' is unclear; twenty is expressed by ''buŋte or buŋdaluk te'', 'man one'. Nowadays, people only use the traditional numbers from 1 to 5. They would mostly use English (Tok Pisin) these days.
