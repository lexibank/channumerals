The Chibchan Language Family

  1. Aruak: Cogui, Malayo, Arhuaco
  2. Chibchan Proper:
        Barro Negro Tunebo, Western Tunebo,
        Angosturas Tunebo, Central Tunebo
  3. Cofan: Cofán
  4. Guaymi: Ngäbere, Bulgere
  5. Kuna: San Blas Kuna, Border Kuna
  6. Motilon: Barí
  7. Paya: Pech
  8. Rama: Maléku Jaika, Rama
  9. Talamanca: Boruca, Bribri, Cabécar, Teribe
  10. Unclassified: Chimila
