Notes:

The palatal fricatives [ʃ] and [Ʒ] are free variants of the alveolar fricatives [s] and [z].

The pharyngeal fricative [ʕ] occurs as an off-glide vowels in certain positions. 

The vowels are also pharyngealized.

The velar fricatives [χ] and [ɣ] are allophones of the velar stops [k] and [g].
