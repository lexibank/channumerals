Other comments:  The Sakizaya (native name: Sakuzaya, literally "real man"; Chinese: 
 撒奇萊雅族; pinyin: Sāqíláiyǎ; occasionally Sakiraya or Sakidaya) are Taiwanese Aborigines with a population of approximately 5,000–10,000. They primarily live in the cities/counties of Keelung, Taoyuan City, and New Taipei, as well as on Hualien (formerly known as Kiray), where their culture is centered.  The Sakizaya numeral system is similar to that of the Northern dialect of the Amis language.
