Other comments: Ye'kwana has a quinary-vigesimal system. Note that: amo or amë= 'hand', amohato or amehato, -hato is a sufix. de'wë or deːwë is postposition means ''upon''. amohadëto de wë toːni is literally ''1 upon 10''. ''hu''=foot, so 15 huhatano could be refer to one foot, -no is a nomalizer. ''soto''=person.
