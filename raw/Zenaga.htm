Other comments: There are feminine and masculine forms for numerals from one to ten in Zenaga. Note the following conversion between traditional symbols and IPA transcriptionsː

1. ''barred d'' =IPA [ð], 2. š = IPA [ʃ]

3. y = IPA [j], 4. ä = IPA [æ], 5. ī = IPA [iː]

6. ḍ = IPA [dˤ], 7. ẓ = IPA [zˤ]
