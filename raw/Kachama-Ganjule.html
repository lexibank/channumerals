Other comments: Ganjule has a decimal system of counting similar to that of Zayse-Zargulla. The linker -ne in the compound numbers means 'and'. The number for 'thousand' are borrowed from Amharic.

Note that the symbol <č> is a voiceless palatal affricative, IPA <tʃ>, <š> is <ʃ>.
