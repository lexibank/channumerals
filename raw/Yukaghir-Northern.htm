Other comments: Northern Yukaghir or Tundra Yukaghir has a decimal system different from that of Southern Yukaghir or Kolyma Yukaghir. Tundra Yukaghir has different variants for the numerals 11-19 and 21-29. There are Russian loanwords for hundred and thousand.
