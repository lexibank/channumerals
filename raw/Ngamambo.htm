Other comments: Ngamambo has a decimal system.There are three words for a thousand. They are: tʃùʔ fíbì, or fíbì, or tɔ́sɨ̀n fíbì. tɔ́sɨ̀n is borrowed from the English word ‘thousand’ and is mostly used when referring to money. In counting, kám is used in the majority of cases. 10000 is tʃûʔ-rɨ̀-ɣum, 100000 is tʃûʔ-rɨ̀-kɨfiβì.
