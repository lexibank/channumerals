Other comments: Vagla has a vigesimal system with special construction for 6 and 9. When counting, number one become / k͡péé/ and the /a/ is dropped from numbers 2 - 7. For counting people, /b/ is add to the beginning of numbers 2 - 7ː (banaa, bahoro, etc). For 50, there is a loanword from the Gonja language. Tone markingsː /á/= High, /à/= Low, Mid tone (unmarked).
