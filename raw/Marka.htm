Other comments: Marka, like Bambara, has an old numbering system based on 80 rather than 100. This changed most likely with the introduction of money. The first set is old system and the second the new one. In traditional system, 70 is expressed by '80, less ten' The new numbers are increasingly influenced by Jula, a trade language related to Bamabara. Noteː the 'n' at the end of  a word such as 'kyen' and 'kondon' indicates nasality.
