Other comments: Makhuwa-Marrevone has a quinary, decimal numeral system. Note that /m̩/ and /n̩/ are syllabic nasals, /j/  = voiced palatal approximant.  ˈ= stress and ˌ= secondary stress. High tone = /á/ and Low tone is unmarked.
