Other comments: Lukpa is a Gur language, of Gurunsi group, a variant (dialect) of Kabyɛ or Kabiye. Lukpa, also called lǝkpa or lokpa or lugba, is spoken in Bénin while Kabyɛis spoken in Togo. Some Lukpa numerals are similar to that of Kabiye. Note that 8: pǝ́lé  'two' probably borrowed from Gurma languages; fɛ́jɩ́ 'missing'.
