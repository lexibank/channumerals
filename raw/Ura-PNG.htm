Other comments: Ura has a complex counting system similar to that of Qairaq.

Note: The numbers 1-4 are not referring to the fingers on the hand, even though the whole numbering system is a base 5. The Uramät or Ura-speaking Baining people have names for the five fingers apart from the numbering system.

The numbers 1, 2 and 3/plural have complexity as previously submitted.

The words 5 = 'one hand', 10 = 'two hand'. 15 = 'two hands plus one foot',

20 = 'an erect (whole) man, 25 = 'ɑ.mɑ ɾu.ʏɑ ɑ mɑ ɻək.təm.ɡɑ dɑ ɡɑ.ɾɑ.ɛt',
