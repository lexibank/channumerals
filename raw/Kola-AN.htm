Other comments: Kola has has a decimal system with special substructure for 8 which

is formed by 'four two', which is similar to that of Dobel language and other Aru languages. The numbers  hundred and thousand are loanwords from Malay.

100,000 is liɸʊn hat 'ɔt, 1,000.000 is d͡ʒuta 'ɔt. The data is from Gomsey dialect.

Note that in Kola language, /f/ and /ɸ/ are two separate / different phonemes.
