Other comments: Ditammari has a decimal system.

1. Agreement with cardinals and ordinals is different. - cardinals: the class markers are
    prefixed to them: "ī-bí ī-nàà ‘4 children', sī-cyɛ̃́ ‘5 houses'

2. When numerals are used as modificators of nouns, the units are in agreement with

    the Class marker of the noun: bā-nìì-bà tāpíítà nà bā-dyā ‘2 hommes'

3. The numerals from 1-10, 100 and 1000 can also be considered basic words.
