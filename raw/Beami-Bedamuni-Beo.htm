Other comments: Beami or Bedamuni have a symmetrical body-part tally system with a cycle length of 33 and a mid-point at the nose. Noun modifiers are afai, afadafa, -isu for 1, aduna, elela for 2 and udiana for 3.

Anthropologists Peter Dwyer and Monica Minnegal recorded the Bedamuni counting system during two months in 1996-97. They recorded the mid-point [17] as mi; mi is an alternative name for nose. They recorded noun modifiers as afae for 1, aduna for 2, udiana for 3, biaduale for 4, biale for 5 and, thereafter, name of body part with -ale as suffix.
