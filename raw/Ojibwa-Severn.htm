Standard lexicon orthography for this language is:
/c/ for /tʃ/, /sh/ for /ʃ/, /ii/ for /iˑ/, /e/ for /eˑ/, /aa/ for /aˑ/, /oo/ for /oˑ,
/hs / is realized as [sˑ]; following /V:/ as [s],  /hʃ / is realized as [ʃˑ],
/a/ is realized as [ʌ], /i/ is realized as [ɪ],  /nk/ is realized as [ŋk],
/p t tʃ k s ʃ/ are voiced in environment /VCV/; voiceless in
environment /VhCV/ and breathgroup initial and final. /Vn/ in env.
/Vnh/, /vns/, /Vnʃ/ is realized as nasalized [ṽ]
