Other comments: Tehuelche has a decimal system. There are masculine and feminine forms for numbers 2 and 3, and two options for number 2, masculine form xawke or xaːuke; feminine form xaone or xaːone. Numbers ' hundred' and 'thousand' are loanwords from the Mapudungun (Mapuche) language.
