Other comments: Bera has a traditional numeral system, but they also use Swahili numbers. Usage of the two forms for 'one'ː with inanimate nounsː the numerals are site above, with animate nouns as followsː

mikí ᵑɡílini - one child.   mikí ɓí-motí - a certain child.

ɓaníkí ɓá-ɓalí - 2 children.  ɓaníkí ɓá-ɓá-sáalɔ - 3 children.

ɓaníkí ɓá-íini - 4 children. From 5 onwards, numerals are invariable.

Please note that there are some prenasalised stops [ ᵑɡ ], [ᵐb], [ᵐp] and [ᶮtʃ].
