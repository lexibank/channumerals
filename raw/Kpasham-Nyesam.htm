Other comments: Nyesam (Kpashan or Kpasham) has a quinary-vigesimal numeral system similar to that of Bali (Maya) language. Numbers 23-29, and 400 or 800 are presumably created in accordance to that of 21 and 22, or 100 and 200, but they have not been recorded. 1000000: kánṍ kób kóp tām ká nṍ.

Note that this is the preliminary list of numeral forms in Nyesam and that the morpho-phonological analysis of the language is not completed and perhaps there are changes and updated analysis that will be included in the near future.
