Other comments: The Paza (pa³³ za³³) language is spoken by approximately 2,100 people in eight villages in Ban Phusang Mai, Muang Samphan, Phongsaly province, with just one village in Oudomxay district.  The group is more broadly known by the

name Phosang, the origin of which is unclear, the Phousang are situated within the Akha group, but the Paza are quite clear that their language, customs and history are not Akha. For now, Paza seems to be closely to Sila, Kheu, Muji and the Bi-Ka languages of China.
