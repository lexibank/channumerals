Other comments: Kalanguya has a decimal system. Kalanguya (Kayapa) is listed in Ethnologue as Kayapa Kallahan. However, native speakers use the name “Kalanguya” when referring to their language and ethnic group. The language is also spoken in municipalities of Ambaguio, Aritao and Sta.Fe, Nueva Vizcaya. Note that the 'n' in 200 and 800 is the linker [ni].
