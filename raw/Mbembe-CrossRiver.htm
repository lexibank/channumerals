Other comments: Cross River Mbembe has a vigesimal numeral system with basic numerals 5, 10, 15, 20 and 400. Numbers between 20 and 400 are generated as multiples of 20. The traditional highest term is 400 ''ɛ́nɔhná''. 1000 is expressed by ( 400 x 2 plus 20 x 10 ). There are lenis and fortis consonants;  t̩, k̩, s̩ and f̩ are fortis. The basic tone system comprises two tone levels, High [á] and Low [à] and a downstep . Note the symbols used between traditional and IPA areː  y = IPA [ j ],  j = [d͡ʒ] and ch = IPA [t͡ʃ ].
