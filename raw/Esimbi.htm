Other comments: Esimbi is NOT a Tivoid language, though that is where it is currently classified. It is definitely Niger-Congo, but appears to be unrelated to any languages

which surround it.

Esimbi has a decimal system.

Noun class markers are given in the citation form of numbers. Numbers agree with the head noun when in a noun phrase.
