Other comments: Fallam Chin has a decimal system. There are four tones in Falam Chinː Low is unmark; High is marked with an acute; Raising with a circumflex [v̂ ] and Failing with a carat [v̌ ]. Please note that the symbol [ʡ] is a voiced epiglottal plosive and there is a single word for ''twenty''.
