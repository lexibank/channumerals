Other comments: With some differences in tones, the numerals of Khuen are identical to those of Shan. The phonetic realization of the tones is described using Chao (1934)

system which used a 5-point scale with 1-low and 5-high. The tones as are follows:

Tone 1: 25; Tone 2: 32, Tone 3: 12; Tone 4: 22: Tone 5:33: tone 6: 52.
