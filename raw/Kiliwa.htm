Other comments: Kiliwa has a complex decimal system with special substructure for numbers 6 to 9. 11 't͡ʃpam=msir=msir-t=ʔ-mal=haa' means ''ten plus one''.

100 is 't͡ʃpam=msir-uʔkun=yuu-t=t͡ʃpam=msir',

200 is 't͡ʃpam=xwaq uʔkun=yuu t=čpam=msir',

1,000 is 'čpam=msir-uʔkun=yuu-uʔ-kun=yuu-t''.

Note that the traditional phonetic symbol 'č', a palatal affricate, =IPA[ t͡ʃ ].
