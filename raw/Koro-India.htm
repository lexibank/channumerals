Other comments: Koro or Koro Aka has a decimal numeral system. Data taken from Roger Blench, " The Koro Language of Arunachal Pradeshː Wordlist and Etymological Analysis", Cambridge, 26 March, 2018. The Koro is a newly discovered language spoken by a small community in Arunachal Pradesh of North-East India. This language is first discovered by me in 2005.Koro is marginally tonal and tones are marked as heard. However, it is likely there are just two contrastive word melodies, Low and High. Even these may be predictable, although a larger sample of lexemes will be needed to be certain of this.
