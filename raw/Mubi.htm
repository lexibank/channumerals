Note on the consonantsː voiced plosives and implosives are realized as voiceless in

word final positions, so the voiceless bilabial plosive [p] occurs word finally for /b/

and /ɓ/. The voiceless fricatives /ʃ/ (postalveolar) and /x/ (velar) occur in words

borrowed from Arabic. The glottal [ʔ] can also occur word medially in Arabic loan

words. The glottal also appears to occur regularly before a word initial vowel.
