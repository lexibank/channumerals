Notesː
  1. /v¹/ in Rigwe language is not a labio-dental flap as described in the International Phonetic Alphabet revised 2005,  but a bilabial flap. The use of this IPA symbol is due to lack of an IPA symbol for the sound.

  2. /j̥ʷ²/ and /jʷ/ in the IPA are /ɥ̥ /respectively. However, they are classified as labial-palatal consonants rather that palatal.
